Why Brew Break

A coffee app with a point of view.

Most coffee maps are just directories. Brew Break should feel more curated, more social, and more emotionally specific — something between a city guide, a planning tool, and a premium lifestyle app.

☕

Moment-led

Open the app and pick the mood first, so the experience feels purposeful instead of generic.

✦

Curated

Less clutter, more taste. Highlight places that feel right for a catch-up, work block, or reset.

↗

Social

Built around making plans with friends, not just browsing café pins on a map by yourself.

Core experience
Choose a coffee vibe the way you’d choose a dinner mood.

The first screen should immediately explain the product: not “find cafés near me,” but “what kind of coffee moment are you looking for?” That framing makes Brew Break feel distinct from a standard locator.

Examples
Quick catch-up, solo reset, work break, first meet.

These are stronger than generic categories because they translate directly to real plans.

Design direction
Warm neutrals, glassy layers, soft brown depth.

This palette gives Brew Break its own identity instead of feeling like Sip Sober in a new category.
